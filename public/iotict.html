Our Services

Smart Gear Integration

iClimb integrates IoT sensors seamlessly into climbing gear, transforming traditional equipment into smart, data-driven assets. Harnesses, ropes, carabiners, and other essential gear are embedded with sensors that monitor crucial metrics such as tension, stress, and temperature in real-time. This data is transmitted wirelessly to climbers' devices, providing instant insights into equipment condition and performance.

Route Planning & Navigation

Navigate with confidence using iClimb's advanced ICT-powered route planning and navigation tools. Our platform offers dynamic mapping features that analyze terrain, weather conditions, and user preferences to suggest optimal routes. Climbers can access detailed route information, including difficulty levels, hazards, and recommended strategies, enhancing safety and efficiency in challenging environments.

Monitoring & Alert Systems

Safety is paramount in climbing, and iClimb prioritizes it with state-of-the-art safety monitoring and alert systems. IoT sensors continuously monitor vital signs, movement patterns, and environmental conditions, detecting potential risks such as falls, exhaustion, or sudden changes in weather. In the event of an emergency, automatic alerts are sent to designated contacts or rescue services.
