Frontend Web Developer - MERN STACK

Location : Gurugram

Experience : 3+ years

About Us:

iClimb is a dynamic and innovative tech company dedicated to revolutionizing the way people connect and engage in the climbing community. We are passionate about outdoor adventure and strive to create intuitive digital solutions that enhance the climbing experience for enthusiasts worldwide. Join us in our mission to empower climbers and outdoor adventurers through cutting-edge technology.

Position Overview:

We are seeking a talented Frontend Web Developer with expertise in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) to join our team. As a Frontend Web Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining our web applications, ensuring a seamless user experience and responsive design across various devices. You will collaborate closely with our backend developers, designers, and product managers to translate requirements into high-quality, scalable solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Develop and implement user-friendly web interfaces using modern frontend technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React.js.
  • Work closely with designers to translate UI/UX design wireframes into code, ensuring technical feasibility and optimal performance.
  • Collaborate with backend developers to integrate frontend components with server-side logic and APIs.
  • Optimize web applications for maximum speed and scalability, ensuring cross-browser compatibility and responsive design.
  • Implement and maintain best practices for code quality, security, and performance optimization.
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging frontend technologies and trends, recommending improvements and enhancements to the development process.
  • Participate in code reviews, debugging, and troubleshooting to resolve issues and ensure smooth deployment of features.
  • Contribute to the overall architecture and design of web applications, providing valuable insights and solutions to technical challenges.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • Proven experience as a Frontend Web Developer with expertise in the MERN stack.
  • Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), and React.js.
  • Experience with state management libraries such as Redux or MobX.
  • Solid understanding of responsive design principles and frontend build tools like Webpack.
  • Knowledge of version control systems (e.g., Git) and agile development methodologies.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Passion for outdoor adventure and familiarity with the climbing community is a plus.
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary and performance-based incentives.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options.
  • Generous vacation and paid time off.
  • Professional development opportunities and training programs.
  • Fun and dynamic work environment with regular team outings and activities.
